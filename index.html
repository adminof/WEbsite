About GEHRI

Who We Are

GEHRI Foundation is a registered non-profit organization working towards environmental conservation, community development, and sustainable social impact. Rooted in grassroots action and driven by responsibility, we focus on creating long-term change through practical, community-led initiatives.

Why We Exist

GEHRI Foundation exists to carry forward a legacy of nurturing nature and protecting life. Inspired by the values of our founder’s grandmother, GEHRI, who believed in planting trees and preserving the environment, we aim to transform personal responsibility into collective impact.

Our Values

Transparency, community engagement, environmental stewardship, and measurable impact guide every action we take.
